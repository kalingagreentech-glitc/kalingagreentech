🛠 Step-by-step Guide

Solar Installation Process

From your first enquiry to a fully commissioned solar system - here's exactly what happens at every stage when you go solar with Kalinga GreenTech

Book a Free Assessment Calculate Your Cost
📋

Step 01

Assessment & Planning

Before any work begins, we evaluate your property, energy patterns, and goals to design the perfect system for your needs.

  • ✓ Check roof space - shadow-free area and correct orientation (south-facing ideal)
  • ✓ Analyse monthly electricity usage and peak consumption hours
  • ✓ Recommend the right system type: On-Grid (grid-tied, lower cost) or Off-Grid (with battery backup)
⏱ Typical Duration: 1-2 Days
📝

Step 02

Approvals & Documentation

Our team handles all the paperwork and official approvals so you don't have to worry about compliance or permits.

  • ✓ Apply for government subsidy and net metering registration
  • ✓ Conduct site feasibility check by certified engineers
  • ✓ Obtain official approval from local DISCOM / electricity board to proceed
⏱ Typical Duration: 5-15 Days
🔧

Step 03

Installation

Our trained installation team sets up your complete solar system efficiently and safely - from the mounting frame to the final wiring.

  • ✓ Install galvanised mounting structure suited to your roof type
  • ✓ Fix and secure solar panels on the mounting frame
  • ✓ Connect all DC/AC wiring, solar inverter, and battery (for off-grid systems)
⏱ Typical Duration: 1-3 Days
?

Step 04

Testing & Commissioning

Before handover, every component is thoroughly tested and inspected to ensure safe, reliable, and optimised performance.

  • ✓ Complete safety inspection of all electrical connections and components
  • ✓ Install bi-directional net meter for grid-export measurement
  • ✓ Full system test, live activation, and customer walkthrough & handover
⏱ Typical Duration: 1 Day
