Privacy Policy

At Kalinga Greentech, your trust matters. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information across our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that helps us deliver a better experience and respond more effectively to your requests.

  • •Personal details (name, email, phone number) when you fill out forms or contact us.
  • •Technical data (IP address, browser type, device information).
  • •Usage data (pages visited, time spent, and interactions).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to support your journey with solar energy and keep our website working smoothly.

  • •Respond to inquiries and provide solar solutions.
  • •Improve website performance and user experience.
  • •Send updates or promotional messages (only with your consent).

3. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance functionality and analyze traffic. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser or our Cookie Policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may use trusted third-party services like Google Analytics or WhatsApp chat to improve our site.

5. Data Security

We use SSL encryption and secure servers to protect your information from unauthorized access.

6. Your Rights

You can request to access, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting us at info@kalingagreentech.in.

7. Updates

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on our website.
